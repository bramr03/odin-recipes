Negroni recipe

On this page you will find the recipe for the perfect negroni cocktail!

If you follow this perfectly, you will have an amazing traditional Italian negroni!

Ingredients:

  • Gin
  • Campari
  • Sweet vermouth
  • Garnish: orange peel

Steps:

  1. Add 1 ounce of all the alcoholic beverages to a mixing glass
  2. Add ice and stir until chilled
  3. Strain into a cocktail glass of your desire
  4. Garnish your negroni with an orange peel
  5. Enjoy!
