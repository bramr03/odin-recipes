Lasagna recipe

On this page you will find this recipe for the perfect lasagna!

If you follow this perfectly, you will have an amazing traditional Italian lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • Minced meat
  • Vegetables
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Seasoning

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200 degrees Celcius
  2. Season the meat and cook it until perfection
  3. Cut the vegetables into pieces and mix with the tomato sauce and the minced meat
  4. Add a layer of lasagna sheets and put the sauce on top
  5. Repeat step 4 until there is no more sauce left
  6. Put in the oven for 30 minutes
  7. Let it cool down and enjoy
