Tiramisu recipe

On this page you will find this recipe for the perfect tiramisu!

If you follow this perfectly, you will have an amazing traditional Italian tiramisu!

Ingredients:

  • Long finger cookies
  • Mascarpone
  • Eggs
  • Cacao powder
  • Ammaretto
  • Coffee

Steps:

  1. Mix the mascarpone and the yolks of the egg
  2. Dip the long finger cookies in the ammarretto and coffee mix
  3. Put down a bottom of the soaked cookies and add the mascarpone mix on top
  4. Put the cacao powder on top of the tiramisu
  5. Put the tiramisu dessert into the fridge
  6. Let it rest for 2 hours
  7. Take it out and enjoy
